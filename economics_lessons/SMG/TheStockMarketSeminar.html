suggested goals

Coaches: You will be assessing your partner.
  • Cited reasons and evidence for statements
  • Provided relevant and insightful comments
  • Spoke loudly and clearly
  • Stayed focused on the conversation
  • Invited others to the discussion
  • Shared air time equally with others
  • Moved the discussion to a deeper level

Question Focus:

"you have to spend money to make money."

questions from our class

  1. Why is the stock market so important?
  2. How does the stock market affect the economy?
  3. How does the Stock Market relate to the economic principles of scarcity and opportunity cost?
  4. Is it significant to make the same amount of money you spent or to make more? What is profit?
  5. Why do we use the Stock Market as a measure of the entire economy?
  6. Can you trust a stockbroker or not?
  7. Why do stocks exist in the first place?
  8. Why were stocks created?
  9. Where are the most stocks traded?
  10. What makes a successful investor?
  11. Is the stock market fair?
  12. Do you have to spend money to make money?
  13. How can you spend money to make money?
  14. Why do stars make more money?
  15. What is the most amount of money you can make of a share of stock?
  16. Does money give you power?
